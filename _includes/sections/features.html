From MTL…

Minimum Couette makes everything in Montreal. The fabric is sourced in Canada and in the USA. The duck down is sourced in Canada from ethical producers.

With Love!

All product are developed and tested with enthusiasts, cyclists, hikers, backpackers.

Made Simple & Light…

At Minimum Couette we believe hiking gear must be as light as possible, to help hiker go further.

With Canadian Duck Down

Our quilts are made with Canadian Duck Down, ethically processed by Hutterite communities in the West. The ducks are never live-plucked.
